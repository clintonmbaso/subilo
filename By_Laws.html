DUES/ PENALTIES RELATED TO SAVINGS/LENDING/BORROWING/CONDUCT

1. Failure to deposit savings:
Charge ZMW 1000.00

2. Surety requirement:
If a member seeks to borrow an amount that is more that his or her shareout value, the guarantors account should stand in as surety if his or her shareout value equates the deficit as regards the required money to be borrowed.

3. Minimum loan amount per member per meeting:
ZMW 2,000.00

4. Interest percentage on Loan / Saving:
20%

5. Loan repayment instalment distribution:
Should be repaid by the second last meeting of the cycle.

6. Failure to meet monthly Loan repayment instalment in full (part payment):
ZMW 500.00 charged and added to the amount to be repaid.

7. Outstanding recoveries at end of cycle:
Deducted from savings at share out. If the pending exceeds the take home, the guarantor's account will have to compensate.

8. Failure to make a saving for three (3) consecutive months:
Expulsion from the group.

9. Failure to return the loan’ minimum for three (3) consecutive months:
Expulsion from the group.

10. Unethical conduct:
Expulsion from the group.

11. Refusal to pay back loan (Total Default):
Case forwarded to relevant authorities for restitution.

OTHER ISSUES

1. Executive reserve the right to guide on other incidentals that arise.
